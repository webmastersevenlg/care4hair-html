ARTAS® Robotic Hair Transplant

technique
benefits
ARTAS is a fully robotic Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE) hair transplanting device that operates via computer programming. Its 3-D stereoscopic vision system determines the optimal hairs for grafting by detecting and evaluating hair type, density, and growth angles.
  • Computerized digital imaging for micron-level accuracy
  • Premiere algorithm determines the best follicles to extract
  • Analyzes, monitors, and tracks each hair sixty times per second
  • Calculated distribution and optimal spacing between grafts
more about artas
With ARTAS technology, the thousandth graft maintains the same stellar quality as the first graft.
  • Can harvest up to 1,000 grafts per hour, making it up to 50% faster than other FUE systems
  • Hair is grafted with protective tissue, thus being less susceptible to the drying effects of suction compared to other FUE hair transplant technologies
  • Less risk of damage to surrounding healthy hairs

Neograft® Automated FUE

technique
benefits
NeoGraft is one of the leading implantation technologies utilized when performing a Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE) hair transplant. In place of a scalpel, the semi-automatic Neograft technology comfortably extracts your own individual hair follicles from the donor site to be reimplanted in balding recipient areas.
  • Streamlined process allows surgeons to extract hair follicles with greater precision.
  • Prevents the noticeable scarring caused by conventional hair transplant techniques.
  • NeoGraft uses hair follicles in batches of 1 to 4 units, resulting in a more natural-looking hairline for both men and women.
more about neograft
The FUE process with NeoGraft combines advanced technology with the guided precision of a qualified surgeon.
  • Can be performed on any hair type
  • Customized punch sizes selected by your surgeon according to the nature and quality of your hair
  • Lower transection rates (damage rates) to grafted follicles when performed by an experienced NeoGraft surgeon
  • More affordable than other FUE hair transplant technologies

What Care4Hair Offers You

FUE

FUE hair transplant is a minimally invasive procedure that involves extracting individual hair follicles from a donor part of the body and manually implanting them into balding areas.

PRP

Platelet-rich plasma (PRP) is a treatment that stimulates hair growth. It is also used to help decrease hair loss; usually combined with other hair loss treatments and procedures.
