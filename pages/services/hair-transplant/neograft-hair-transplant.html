Neograft Automated FUE Hair Transplant

NeoGraft is one of the leading Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE) hair transplant technologies. The semi-automatic NeoGraft technology comfortably extracts your healthy hair follicles from the donor area to be reimplanted in the areas of hair loss. The hair specialists at Care4Hair in Miami aim to guide patients through their hair restoration journeys and revive their confidence once more! In the months following the procedure, permanent hairs will begin to grow for a complete and natural-looking hairline.

What Will Happen In My Initial Consultation With The Care4Hair Specialists?

During your initial consultation with Care4Hair, you will be encouraged to share your grievances and medical history. Our doctor will then classify your hair loss and present you with the most effective procedures for your condition. If a NeoGraft hair transplant is the best option, the doctor will move on to the design phase and construct a visual strategy to suit your desired look.

Benefits Of A NeoGraft Hair Transplant

Unlike other hair transplant techniques and technologies, the NeoGraft system grafts follicles of any hair type for optimal results. Whether your hair is dark, light, curly, or straight, a NeoGraft FUE can be performed comfortably to generate positive results. Not only can the doctor customize punch sizes according to the quality of your hair, but the NeoGraft technology yields lower transaction (or damage) rates when performed by a qualified surgeon.

The NeoGraft hair transplant is a quick and effective procedure that yields natural-looking results by combining advanced technology with the guided precision of a qualified surgeon. The process consists of the following steps:

How Many FUE Grafts Will I Need?

Our hair specialists will determine the number of hair grafts you need after your initial evaluation. While it depends on the area size, the average NeoGraft hair transplant procedure will require approximately 1400 to 1600 grafts. Each graft has 1 to 4 hair follicles with an average of 2 follicles per graft. The average patient has approximately 6000 grafts available in their donor area to create the appearance of fullness.

How To Prepare For A NeoGraft Procedure?

In general, a NeoGraft hair transplant surgery takes 3-8 hours. Since you will remain stationary throughout the procedure, Care4Hair recommends you wear loose clothing to avoid discomfort at this time. Also, you should arrange for a ride to and from the Care4Hair clinic. It is natural for some patients to feel a slight discomfort after the NeoGraft hair transplant surgery and having a designated driver is both safer and more convenient.

The Day Of Your NeoGraft Hair Transplant In Miami

The hair specialists at Care4Hair do everything possible to make you feel comfortable on the day of your NeoGraft hair transplant surgery. Our team will first check your vital signs to confirm your preparation for the procedure.

The Care4Hair specialists will shave and disinfect the area before local anaesthetic is applied. Once the skin feels numb, the patient will lay face down while the doctor extracts the healthiest hair follicles in groups of 1 to 4. The NeoGraft machine extracts the hair gently, removing 600 units per hour. After the extraction, you may use the restroom, stretch your legs, and relax. After your break, the doctor carefully transplants the harvested follicles, and the Care4Hair specialists clean and bandage the scalp to conclude.

What Happens After The NeoGraft Procedure Is Performed?

The follicles need to rest after a NeoGraft hair transplant. The hair in the donor area should grow back 7-10 days after the surgery while the transplanted hair follicles remain rooted in the scalp. In the case that you experience minor swelling or discomfort, the doctor will prescribe an appropriate medication.

NeoGraft Results

A NeoGraft FUE hair transplant can take from 12-18 months to reflect full, permanent results. It is important to note that transplanted hair is likely to go through a “shedding phase” and be released after some weeks following the procedure. While some patients do not experience this, most patients’ bodies will enter a resting phase as a natural response to the surgery. The grafts remain healthy and will grow back in the months to follow (considering the patient observes all aftercare instructions).

FAQS

Patients with hair loss caused by stress, trauma, and genetic history are typically fine candidates for a Neograft hair transplant. To undergo this procedure, a patient must also have sufficient donor follicles. Therefore, patients with Diffuse Unpatterned Alopecia are not great candidates for a NeoGraft FUE since they usually do not have enough donor follicles to harvest.

While a NeoGraft FUE can theoretically be performed on patients of any age, we ask that patients be over 30 before considering this procedure. The clinical onset of baldness in both men and women is generally around 30 to 40 years of age. Around this age is when hair loss also begins to stabilize. Since hair loss in patients under 30 fluctuates, it may be difficult to determine how it will stabilize. If a patient undergoes a NeoGraft FUE at an early age, they may need to undergo additional sessions in the future depending on how the hair loss progresses.

The NeoGraft procedure is low risk but since it is a surgery, there is always the possibility of complications. Patients run the risk of bleeding, numbness, discomfort, infection, or allergic reactions to the anesthesia. The Care4Hair clinic will always encourage patients to fully disclose medical histories, current medications, and known allergies to minimize risk. Designated hair specialists will also provide full aftercare instructions to ensure a satisfactory outcome.

The results of the NeoGraft FUE hair transplant procedure are considered permanent. The healthy follicles will grow and yield long-lasting results as long as patients follow our doctor's post-procedure care instructions. Rest assured, the Care4Hair specialists will ensure you have all the necessary information before your surgery and departure. We strongly recommend careful adherence to these instructions for optimal results and overall satisfaction.

Yes. The NeoGraft hair restoration procedure is a suitable technique to treat hair loss in both men and women. In fact, women’s hair loss tends to be more diffuse in comparison to their male counterparts. Therefore, our doctor often recommends the NeoGraft FUE to his female clients, as it allows for a more targeted placement of follicles compared to more traditional methods.

While recovery from a NeoGraft FUE hair transplant is fast, every patient is different. Some patients resume their regular activities almost immediately, while others may take off anywhere from a few days to a week to recover from swelling or discomfort. Most patients only require medication 2-3 days after surgery. The overall healing process does not take longer than 7 days. The donor zone will appear fully healed in about 10 days as hair begins to grow again.

Following the shedding phase that takes place 1-5 weeks following the transplant procedure, healthy hairs will begin to form. Within 3-4 months, hair should begin to grow steadily and final results will appear 12-18 months post-procedure.
